# HR Analytics Project – Employee Attrition Analysis

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition to understand why employees leave an organization. The goal is to identify key attrition patterns across departments and demographics and support data-driven HR decision-making.

## Objectives
- Analyze employee attrition trends across departments  
- Identify key reasons for employee exits  
- Study attrition based on gender and experience levels  
- Support HR teams with actionable insights for retention strategies  

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI  
- Microsoft Excel  

## Key Insights
- Legal and Product Management departments show higher employee exits  
- Better position, preferred job location, and higher studies are the top reasons for attrition  
- Employees with 0–3 years of experience show higher exit rates  
- Gender-based attrition is balanced, indicating role-driven exits  

## Outcome
The project delivers interactive Power BI dashboards that help visualize attrition patterns and enable HR teams to make informed workforce and retention decisions.
